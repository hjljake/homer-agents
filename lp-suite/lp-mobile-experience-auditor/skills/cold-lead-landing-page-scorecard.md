# Cold Lead Landing Page Scorecard — Section 5: Mobile Experience (10 points)

*The majority of cold traffic — especially from paid social and display — is mobile. If the page doesn't work on a phone, your ad spend is wasted.*

| Criteria | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|----------|---|---|---|
| **Load Speed**: Does the page load in under 3 seconds on mobile? Every additional second costs ~7% of conversions. Cold visitors from paid channels have essentially zero patience. | Over 5 seconds | 3-5 seconds | Under 3 seconds |
| **Hero on Mobile**: Is the full hero (headline, subhead, CTA) visible on a phone screen without scrolling? Content should re-stack, not shrink. The CTA must be visible on the first mobile screen. | Hero requires scrolling to see CTA | Most of hero visible but CTA is cut off | Complete hero visible on first mobile screen |
| **Tap Targets**: Are buttons large enough to tap with a thumb? Minimum 48px height (Google), ideally 56px+. Enough spacing between interactive elements to prevent mis-taps. | Small buttons, crowded tap targets | Mostly tappable but some tight spacing | All buttons are thumb-friendly with generous spacing |
| **Readability**: Is text large enough to read without zooming? 16px minimum body text. Line length appropriate for mobile (not spanning full width). | Text too small, lines too long | Readable but not optimized | Perfect readability, comfortable line length, clear hierarchy |
| **Form Friction**: If there's a form, is it minimal? Every field reduces completion. For cold visitors: name + email maximum. Phone number costs ~5% completion per field. Each additional field beyond 2 drops conversion ~10%. | Long form with unnecessary fields (5+) | Reasonable but could be shorter (3-4 fields) | Minimal fields: only what's absolutely necessary (1-2 fields) |
