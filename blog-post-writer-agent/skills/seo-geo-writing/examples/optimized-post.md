## 1. Freemium Pricing
> [SEO] H2 with secondary keyword. Numbered for scanability.

**Freemium pricing is a SaaS model where a basic version of the product is offered for free, with paid tiers for advanced features.** It's used by 38% of SaaS companies with ARR over $10M (OpenView 2025).
> [GEO] Definition in "X is Y" format — first sentence. Specific stat immediately after. Both are highly extractable.

### When Freemium Works
> [SEO] H3 subsection. Phrased for search intent ("when does freemium work").

Freemium works best when:
- Your product has natural viral loops (Slack, Dropbox)
- The free tier creates switching costs
- Your addressable market is large enough that 2-5% conversion supports the business

> [GEO] Bullet list — easily extractable by AI engines.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most popular SaaS pricing model?
**Tiered pricing is the most common SaaS pricing model, used by 49% of SaaS companies** according to the 2025 Paddle Pricing Report. It's followed by per-seat (24%) and usage-based (18%).
> [GEO] Question as H3. Bold definitive answer. Cited source. AI engines will surface this directly.

### How often should you change SaaS pricing?
**Most SaaS companies should revisit pricing every 6-12 months.** Companies that review pricing quarterly grow 20% faster than those that set it once (Price Intelligently, 2024).
> [GEO] Same pattern: direct answer, specific data, named source.
